Plastic Love

Original Artists: Mariya Takeuchi / 竹内 まりや

Arranged by: Yoo shin Tanai '23

Cast: Everyone... It's the Company Piece

Description: This is my first ever acapella arrangement for VTone! It's been so amazing to see the piece come to life with everyone's beautiful voices in harmony. It's a Japanese city pop bop that'll have you on your feet, dancing to the plastic beat. The song became a meme back in '17 and has been parodied countless times since!
