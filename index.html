Plastic Love

Original Artists: Mariya Takeuchi / 竹内 まりや

Arranged by: Yoo shin Tanai '23

Cast: Everyone... It's the Company Piece

Description: This is my first ever acapella arrangement for VTone! It's been so amazing to see the piece come to life with everyone's beautiful voices in harmony. It's a Japanese city pop bop that'll have you on your feet, dancing to the plastic beat. The song became a meme back in '17 and has been parodied countless times since!

BTS Medley

Original Artists: BTS

Songs Included: (Boy With Luv (작은 것들을 위한 시), DNA, Blood Sweat & Tears (피 땀 눈물), Fake Love, I Need U, Spring Day (봄날), Epiphany)

Arranged by: Christina Im '22, Patricia Chen '20

Cast: Christina Im (Soprano), Chloe Chen (Soprano), Mina Yu (Alto), Aneesha Manocha (Alto), Emiri Morita (Alto), Pang Nganthavee (Tenor), Ryan Xia (Tenor), George Huang (Bass), Yoo Shin Tanai (Bass)

Description: straight from pre-covid vtone, 11 straight minutes of bangtan bops. the story of this piece moves from the butterflies of first love to heartbreak and then back to the real message: LOVE YOURSELF. especially to my graduated co-arranger patricia. hope we make you and the rest of ARMY proud.
